Clustering BST Infrastructures

May 05, 2018 | by Hong-Linh Truong

One example of big data analytics is to look at the behavior of stations based on different parameters.

What you will learn:
  • Develop big data analytics based on PySpark and Hadoop FS
  • Data mining with clustering algorithms
Data and systems for tutorial:
  • Data is stored in Hadoop file systems
  • Cluster programs are written with PySpark
The analytics brings the following benefits
  • Cluster and identify stations based on alarms
  • Cluster and identify stations based on their geographical and alarm similarity
