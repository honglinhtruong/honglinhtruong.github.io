My research interests are various fields pertained to distributed systems and distributed computing with a systems-oriented focus. My main research interest focuses Software, Data and Service Engineering Analytics by obtaining an understanding of the behaviour and quality of distributed and parallel applications and systems through monitoring and analysis.
My research has been applied to: Monitoring, Analysis and Optimization Techiques for Programs, Data and Systems; Parallel, Grid and Cloud Computing, and IoT; Data Service Models and Analytics; Socio-technical Services Engineering; and Elastic Computing. Furthermore, i am interested in (free) ICT solutions for (under) developing countries.

See my Research Awards, Team, Publications, Invited Talks and Tutorials, Professional Activities, Research Projects and Software Prototypes

I have involved in teaching activities in different universities including HoChiMinh City University of Technology, University of Vienna, University of Innsbruck, and TU Wien. My teaching is focused on advanced research-oriented topics with a strong emphasis on learning through the development and implementation of projects and new ideas.

I am interested in working with teams of colleagues and students from multi-cultural background. I also like helping students from developing countries with a hope that they eventually would play a major role in transforming the education systems in their countries.

See my current open topics for students, doctoral colleges, teaching courses , PhD/master students, PhD committee membership and research interns and visitors.

As part of my applied research activities, i also perform consulting activities in my core expertise. My consulting work is centered around system design and evaluation for different application domains in collaborative working environments, smart cities, distributed enterprises, large-scale distributed infrastructures, and e-science.

In the past, i did participate in building interconnecting networks and high performance computing systems for Vietnamese universities, feasiblity studies and designs for collaborative systems for Eurospace Agency (ESA), satellite image processing design for ESA, and smart cities middleware for Pacific Control Systems.

My consulting activities are currently focused on elastic computing systems, IoT analytics, big data analytics and smart city analytics. I am also interested in transferring techniques/knowledge from developed countries to (under)developing ones.
